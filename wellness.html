Our Symbol: A Reflection of Universal Truth

The logo for The Divine Planet: Soul Awakening is a powerful visual representation of our core philosophy. Our mission is universally applicable: to guide everyone on the transformative path of Meditation toward harmony, peace, and wellness.

1. The Golden Circle: The Infinite Cosmos

The outermost Golden Circle represents The Infinite Cosmos and the ultimate reality from which all creation flows. It symbolizes the boundless energy that contains all existence, reminding us that we are all held within this single, eternal whole.

2. The Six-Pointed Star: Divine Union and Balance

The vibrant six-pointed star at the center is a universal symbol of Divine Union. It signifies the harmonious interweaving of the material and spiritual realms, heaven and earth. This balance is the essence of true holistic wellness.

3. The Central Eye & Crescent: Inner Self and Awareness

The stylized Eye above a Crescent Moon at the center represents the Inner Self and Consciousness that resides within every individual. The Eye signifies the enlightenment and insight gained through Meditation, while the crescent moon symbolizes growth and the journey of awakening the soul to its full potential.

5. The Name: Divine Planet & Soul Awakening

Our name affirms that all life is sacred (**Divine Planet**), and its inhabitants are here to realize their true purpose. **Soul Awakening** is our clear mission: to guide you back to your authentic, spiritual self and live in harmony with the universal order, regardless of your background or path.
